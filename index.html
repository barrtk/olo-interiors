About

Hi, my name is Oliwier, and I'm a passionate joiner with 12 years of hands-on experience crafting bespoke solid-wood furniture that stands the test of time. Based in Glasgow, I’m happy to take on projects both locally and further afield. I specialize in custom kitchens, wardrobes, shelving, and much more, with each piece carefully designed and built to match your exact vision. Woodworking isn’t just my trade—it’s my lifelong passion. I put my heart into every detail to ensure the end result is heirloom-quality furniture that will last for years to come. From elegant staircases to stunning floors, I take pride in transforming your ideas into functional, beautiful pieces of art. No project is too big or too small, and I’m dedicated to exceeding your expectations with every creation. If you're ready to bring your ideas to life, get in touch with me today for a no-obligation chat. I’d love to help turn your vision into reality.
